Moderate Limber Lost Tour

Our "Moderate Limber Lost Tour" is a fun and rigourus advanced route! Newer cyclists or those who have had some time off the saddle will probably find the pace a bit challenging. This ride is for intermediate level riders or competitive cyclists looking for a break from their regular group race training rides, typically what most clubs would define as a “B” ride.
A breezy 43.01 miles
Only $35 a person

Camino de Santiago 4-Day Tour

Our "Camino de Santiago 4-Day Tour" is a more serious, advanced route for seasoned riders only. You must have been at least on 2 Moderate Tours to be admitte, as seats are limited and the tour is 4 days long and we want to be sure everyone is able to keep up. Our goal is to keep everyone together, but these are not “no drop” rides. There will not be a “sweep” rider and the group will not wait for those who find their fitness or ability prevent them from catching up fairly quickly at the regroup spots.
A breezy 41.2 miles
Only $60 a person
